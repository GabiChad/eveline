What specific aspects of open-mindedness do I aim to address?

Open-mindedness is more than an abstract concept.
It's a deeply continuous process.
It's about being receptive to new realities, experiences, and possibilities. It is also a cultural shared space that has no form or shape, area or volume. This ambiguous space behaves as a liminal mental environment in the forms of realizations, conflicts, individual and collective efforts and exchanges.
